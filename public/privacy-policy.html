Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Heritage Realtor Nigeria ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use heritagerealtors.ng, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Enquiry details: name, phone number, email address, and message when you submit a property enquiry or contact form.
  • Usage data: pages visited and general browsing behaviour, used only to improve the site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to property enquiries and contact you about listings you're interested in.
  • To improve our website and the properties we offer.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

Enquiry information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized Heritage Realtor staff. We retain enquiry records for as long as needed to provide our services and respond to your request.

Your Rights

You may request that we delete your personal information or stop contacting you at any time by emailing us at the address below.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, reach us at info@heritagerealtors.ng or via the contact form on our site.
